When spotify announced their new 1000 stream minimum for monetization, it made me realize that paying for a distribution subscription and abiding by the rules a streaming service sets is 1. not fun and 2. no longer viable for me. With this knowledge in mind, I deleted my distrokid account and decided to find a new way to share my art with the world. I plan to release my music under this website for the foreseeable future. There's ads on this website because I would like to use this website as a way to get revenue from my music while keeping it available and completely free to download for all. Thank you to everyone who's stuck it out with me through these past few months, It's been a very weird transition.
