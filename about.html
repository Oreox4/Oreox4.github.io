I’ve been making music nonstop since I was 11. I have been dealing with a lot of issues recently when it comes to imposter syndrome, and lack of confidence in myself and my own music. I once recently found releasing my music to be pointless due to the constant expectations and criticism i put myself through.

When spotify announced their change to revenue (requiring a minimum of 1000 plays to acquire streaming revenue) i realized that streaming companies will never have the artist’s best interest in front of a bottom line, so i decided to do my own thing, not knowing what that would be yet, but knowing I’d figure something out eventually

This website is an attempt to break free from those thoughts and be creatively free. Everything leads to private soundcloud links where you can stream and download my discography, and expect new stuff soon!

Hopefully the ads I place on this website could replace streaming revenue, so if you’re inclined I’d reallly appreciate it if you disabled your ad blocker, but if you don’t I understand

Thanks for supporting me through these times, you all mean the world to me. - Oreox4, December 15th, 2023 HOME
